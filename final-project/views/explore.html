MonoMuse brings together art, technology, and creativity in one space. Every visit is built around new media, interactive work, and exhibitions that make people look at art in a different way.

Visitors can walk through digital galleries, learn about artists using new tools, and experience exhibits that mix screens, sound, projection, and design.
